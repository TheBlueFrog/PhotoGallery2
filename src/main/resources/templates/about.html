MILKRUN - ABOUT US

To make sustainably grown and raised food available to local communities everywhere

THE MISSION

MilkRun facilitates a direct relationship with farmers and eaters eliminating the middleman and putting more money in the pockets of farmers and bringing savings to customers.

At MilkRun we want customers to have a true farm to door experience by cutting out the middleman and directly connecting farmers and small producers with local buyers. We are committed to making local and sustainable food accessible to everyone. Our orders are fully customizable and with no minimum order we offer a risk-free, hassle-free shopping experience. Working towards sustainability our orders come with no unwanted packaging materials and are delivered in a reusable cooler that keep the products fresh.
